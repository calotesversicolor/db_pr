## Exercise 00 - Move to the LEFT, move to the RIGHT

```
SELECT name, rating
FROM  pizzeria
LEFT  JOIN person_visits ON  pizzeria.id = person_visits.pizzeria_id
WHERE person_visits.pizzeria_id IS NULL
ORDER BY rating DESC;
```
